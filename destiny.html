DESTINY HISTORY

  • DESTINY

    Players take on the role of a Guardian, protectors of Earth's last safe city as they wield a power called Light to protect the City from different alien races.

    RATING: 7/10

  • DARK BELOW

    The expanded story focuses on new character Eris, who has spent years hiding among the Hive only to come forward bearing a warning: The Hive are apparently trying to summon a demigod, Crota, to destroy Earth.

    RATING 6/10

  • HOUSE OF WOLVES

    The Guardians have grown strong, and now the Queen of the Awoken has called upon them to hunt down the House of Wolves, Fallen subjects once loyal to the queen before their violent rebellion.

    RATING: 5/10

  • TAKEN KING

    It focuses on the continued story of Eris Morn. A new character and enemy type, the Taken will also be added. Oryx, The Taken King, is looking for revenge for his son Crota, but to have his revenge he must create a new army, The Taken.

    RATING: 8/10

  • RISE of IRON

    Rise of Iron is an expansion that plunges into the Iron Lords and their ancient fight against the Fallen. You will fight into the heart of the Plaguelands to find SIVA.

    RATING: 6/10
